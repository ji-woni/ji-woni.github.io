About

Jiwon Kim

Hi! I am a PhD candidate in Neuroscience at the University of Pittsburgh, working jointly in Dr. Alberto Vazquez's optical imaging lab and Dr. Sarah Ross's molecular neuroscience lab on an American Heart Association predoctoral fellowship. My research sits at the intersection of vascular biology, in vivo imaging, and circuit neuroscience, and I came to it through six years of multi-institutional work spanning pericyte biology in pulmonary disease and now neurovascular function after stroke. I am most interested in research that stays close to therapeutic development, and my long-term goal is to move into industry where preclinical neurovascular science can be carried toward patients.
